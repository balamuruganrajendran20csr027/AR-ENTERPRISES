About Us

A R Enterprises is a small scale business firm that mainly focusses on import and export of scrap materials. We mainly focus on metal scraps and paper scraps which we do export in a large sclae. The major scrap materials we engage are :
  • Iron scrap
  • Steel scrap
  • Copper scrap
  • Papper scrap
  • Cardboards
  • Electronic scrap

We collect the scraps from the small scale scrap retailers in rural and urban areas from various parts of the state and process them . We do separate the scraps based on their quality,type and make they undergo treatment process to make them better for the industrial purpose. We prioritize customer satisfaction .
